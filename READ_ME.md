# Geopolitical Conflict Risk Dataset

By using Kaggle's Geopolitical Conflict Risk dataset, I applied classification and regression models to determine the factors which most closely predicted that countries would experience conflict within the next six months, and fine-tuned this model to most accurately predict future conflict escalations.
